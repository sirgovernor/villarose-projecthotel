ABOUT US

Villa Rosa Kempinski is located in Westlands, a neighborhood in Nairobi, and is in the entertainment district. Nairobi National Museum and Kenya National Theatre are cultural highlights, and some of the area's landmarks include National Assembly Building and Karen Blixen Coffee Garden and Cottages. American Embassy Memorial Garden and Nairobi Railway Museum are also worth visiting. Discover the area's wildlife on a nearby safari, or try your luck with game drives and game viewing. Some of the service that we offer include:

  • Concierge services.
  • Daily housekeeping.
  • Dry cleaning/laundry service.
  • Tour and ticket assistance.
  • Turndown service.
  • Wedding services.
